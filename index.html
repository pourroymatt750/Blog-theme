About

The Mellvora Timepiece blog is a blog written by the founder of Mellivora Luxury Timepieces, Matthew Pourroy. The goal is to help educate our readers so that when they do actually go pick up a luxury timepiece, whether they buy from Mellivora or a different jeweler, that they will be educated enough to know what to look for in a deal.Due to our founder's reputation, he has written articles for various blogs in the luxury watch space and many of those blog posts wilI be listed below.

Mellivora Luxury Timepieces is a 100% online, luxury watch brokerage where we broker high-end luxury timepieces for our clients at a fraction of the cost of a traditional watch dealer due to reduced overhead.

Matthew Pourroy is a jeweler and a computer programmer, one could say he's a programmer by day and a jeweler by night. In all seriousness, he takes his clients very seriously and has a great reputation in the wonderful world of luxury timepieces.
